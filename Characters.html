Meet the Heroes of Black Myth: Wukong

Discover the legendary characters from Black Myth: Wukong, inspired by the classic novel Journey to the West. Learn about their roles in the game and their storied pasts.

Sun Wukong

In Black Myth: Wukong, Sun Wukong is the main protagonist, a powerful warrior seeking truth and redemption. Known as the Monkey King, he wields immense strength and magical abilities. In the original Journey to the West, Sun Wukong is a rebellious trickster who attains enlightenment after accompanying the monk Xuanzang on a pilgrimage.

Erlang Shen

In the game, Erlang Shen is depicted as a formidable adversary and a deity of immense power. Known as the God of War, he challenges Sun Wukong, testing his abilities and resolve. Erlang Shen wields a three-pointed, double-edged lance and is accompanied by his faithful celestial hound. In the original novel, Erlang Shen is a nephew of the Jade Emperor and is renowned for his third truth-seeing eye and martial prowess, often tasked with subduing rebellious spirits like Sun Wukong.
